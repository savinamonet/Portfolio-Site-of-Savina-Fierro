C-Ware, Inc.

2016

Summary

One of most known and influential fashion designers — Stella McCartney — teamed up with Adidas, to bring the latest styles in sport and casual fashion. MediaMonks produced brand website for new collection from Adidas designed by Stella. Model Karlie Kloss became the face of the campaign.

Problem

My task was to create a subpage for interview with Stella McCartney. The whole website was made to be selling and all pages would lead users to products and invite them to buy them. But the interview page generally consists of questions and answers — so the main problem that occurred is: How to make this page more selling and not make it users end point?

Solution

The pages are setup so that Stella McCartney is always featured on the right side and products on the left. This way we alternate between them and tie Stella’s story to the products she creates. Each product slide has a link back to that specific category. This way users journey always ends in a product discovery.

Design Process

Following the style other pages were made, I had to create a page for interview with Stella McCartney which would fit perfectly into context. I had decided to go with vertically split layout where on one side there would be question and answer, and on the other picture of Stella or her product. This way the page would feel more vibrant and interesting than just displaying plain text.

Role

I worked in a team of designers where art direction and layout were already set. I had to adapt to that style and apply it in my task. I was tasked with creating desktop and mobile page for an interview with Stella McCartney.
