RWC approached monet to start their online presence on the right foot. As a logistic start-up their customer service and prompt delivery set them apart from other local couriers. Business was growing and the owner knew if they wanted to grow even more, they would need an online presence.

Client

RWC Delivery, LLC

Expertise

Website, Branding, Front-End Development

Visit

rwcdelivery.com

Through data analysis and user research, we ascertained what a great courier website needs to do. As a B2C and B2B courier, visitors needed a clear division between services. Wanting to offer a true insight into what services RWC can provide, monet designed a website that funnels visitors into two categories: Residential Services or Commercial Services. The results were a 15% increase in quotes from both B2C and B2B businesses.

A huge part of the redesign was geared around configuring the website from an SEO perspective. We created content specifically to earn key search positions and carried out deep technical optimisation. The results have been staggering. monet continues to work closely with the team at RWC Delivery, helping them grow the brand.
