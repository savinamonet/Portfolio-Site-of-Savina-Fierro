Their website has always been ProGrass’s most important marketing tool, but information was previously very difficult to access. About 66% of their traffic bounced off the home page without looking at any other pages. monet started with a comprehensive web audit of ProGrass's current website. In-depth user research allowed us to build a website that not only looked beautiful, but catered to a variety of needs. From supporting legacy browsers to multiple geolocations, it’s packed full of useful features to help the business grow.

Captivating content is essential for convincing visitors to buy, so everything is written from their point of view. Our talented copywriters wrote every page from scratch based on the needs of the users. And by integrating with market-leading tools like ConstantContact (for marketing automation) and Wordpress (for content management), the website is all geared up to get results and provide a fantastic user experience.
